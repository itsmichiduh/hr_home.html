Caring for your hair in its natural state

Locking in moisture is one of the most beneficial things for 4c hair.

These are ways this can be done:

LOC method
Lotion or leave-in-conditioner followed by oil and cream
LCO method
Lotion or leave-in-conditioner followed by cream and oil
LOCG
Lotion or leave-in-conditioner followed by oil, cream, and gel.
Wearing a bonnet every night or using a silk pillow case
Bed sheets are typically made of cotton and it very drying for the hair and leaves it prone to breakage

The method you choose to follow really comes down to hair porosity

The 3 kinds of porosity are:

High porosity
This means your hair absorbs moisture quickly and also gets dry quickly
Normal porosity
This means your hair absorbs moisture and holds it well
Low porosity
This means your hair has a hard time absorbing moisture and holds on to the moisture it does get

Washday

One of the most essential and challenging things to do with natural hair is washing it. Having a schedule makes it easier to manage wash day because you can plan for it

Sample schedule leading to wash day

Washday schedule
Week Monday Tuesday Wednesday Thursday Friday Saturday Sunday
1 Style Oil scalp Moisturize hair Oil scalp Moisturize hair Wash day or skip till next week
2 Moisturize hair Oil scalp Refresh style if needed with gel
3 Oil scalp Moisturize hair
4 Moisturize hair Oil scalp Refresh style if needed with gel Wash day
This is just an example, do what works best for you
