How do you know if you have 4c hair?

4C hair ranges in tightness of curls. A major indicator is having tightly-coiled and fragile strands with a zig-zag pattern.

Some other indicators are:

  • ‣  Hair struggles to hold moisture
  • ‣  Have extreme shrinkage
  • ‣  Hair is prone to tangles and matting
  • ‣  Hair breaks easily

"Issues" with having 4c hair

The biggest "issue" is 4c hair not being considered "good" hair because it is kinky and not curly, which isn't true because all hair is good hair.

Secondly, maintenance for 4c hair is tedious some may even say an arm workout. It takes as much as 3 to 4 hours from detangling, shampooing, conditioning, deep conditioning, drying, etc.

Shrinkage is an issue for some people, when in actuality, it's a good thing, a sign of healthy hair.

Managing 4c hair can be made easy with protective styling but some damages may occur.
